# Manifestation Codex — Initial Set

Manifestations are the machinery of the ineptocracy made visible: forms,
slogans, debts, redactions, stamps. Filing one means documenting it. Each
entry cites the canon line it embodies. Stats are base values (Resolve /
Pressure / Insulation / Tempo). Dex numbering matches
`game/godot/data/manifestations.json`.

Stages within an evolution line share an entry. Evolution is story/bond-gated
where marked ◆.

---

### 01 RECEIPT → 02 LEDGER → 03 EVIDENCE BOARD — WITNESS
*"Treat every slogan as a receipt. Ask what it bought, who paid for it."*
The starter line of the human network. A Receipt is a small, stubborn fact —
crumpled thermal paper that refuses to fade. As corroboration accumulates it
binds into a Ledger, and finally into the Evidence Board itself: cork, red
string, one desk lamp, connecting MONEY, WAR, MEMORY, MEDIA, OFFICE, GARNIER.
Signature: **Follow the Receipts** (WITNESS, ignores Insulation buffs).
Stats: 44/52/40/56 → 62/72/58/66 → 84/96/80/70. ◆ Ledger→Board requires
20 filed species (the board needs material).
**Visual**: floating paper scrap, ink never dry → ring-bound ledger with
strings beginning to escape the pages → upright cork board, red string,
single hard lamp glow.

### 04 CHECKSUM → 05 COUNTER-HISTORY — WITNESS
*"Their friendship becomes one of the few records the machine cannot easily
rewrite."* / *"a private counter history beneath the geopolitical noise."*
Starter option. A Checksum is shared memory between two people, visualized as
a pair of interlocking amber tally-marks that always match. ◆ Evolves through
bond only (10 arguments fought without being discredited).
Signature: **Cross-Reference** (heals; cures MEMORY status effects).
Stats: 48/44/52/50 → 76/66/82/64.
**Visual**: two luminous tally-strokes orbiting each other → a hand-bound
notebook wrapped in photographs and string, edges singed, contents intact.

### 06 BURNER → 07 ENCRYPTED ROUTE — WITNESS
*"Piercing green eyes, a burner phone, and the rare instinct to help before
the paperwork can forbid it."* / Leah's father left "encrypted routes."
Starter option. The fast, fragile witness: a scuffed prepaid phone, one bar
of signal, numbers memorized not stored. ◆ Evolves after the Keshet route
opens in Act III.
Signature: **One Call** (priority; lets an ally swap without losing a turn).
Stats: 40/50/36/72 → 64/74/56/96.
**Visual**: cheap flip phone with glowing keypad, antenna like a whisker →
a ribbon of cipher-text folding through dark water, harbor lights refracted.

### 08 RUMOR → 09 HEADLINE → 10 NEWS CYCLE — MEDIA
*"Watch which words get repeated."* The smallest media organism. A Rumor is
barely a shape — static with a mouth. Repeated enough, it sets in 96-point
type; industrialized, it becomes a News Cycle, a rotating drum of the same
story wearing new dates.
Signature: **Repetition** (damage rises each consecutive use).
Stats: 38/46/34/64 → 58/68/50/74 → 78/88/68/84.
**Visual**: a smear of broadcast static → a floating front page, headline
legible, body text lorem-blurred → a slow-spinning press drum ringed by
identical glowing chyrons.

### 11 TALKING POINT — MEDIA
*"Watch which words get repeated. Watch which costs are renamed."*
A phrase with handlers. It looks almost reasonable. Common in donor rooms and
green rooms; travels in identical pairs.
Signature: **Stay On Message** (raises own Insulation; never misses).
Stats: 52/58/52/58.
**Visual**: a quotation-mark pair like mandibles around a soft glowing
sentence fragment, kerned too perfectly, slight teleprompter flicker.

### 12 POLISHED CONCERN — MEDIA
*"Sometimes it sounds like concern... That tone can be more dangerous than
open cruelty because it asks readers to relax."*
A lullaby-shaped predator. Lowers your guard before the machine reaches you.
Signature: **Reasonable Tone** (sharply lowers target Pressure).
Stats: 60/44/66/48.
**Visual**: a smooth porcelain mask with a warm studio-lit smile, microphone
foam for a body, hands folded forever.

### 13 ROUGH DRAFT → 14 OFFICIAL STORY → 15 CORRECTED RECORD — MEMORY
*"First the event. Then the story. Then the memory."* /
*"The most dangerous record is the corrected one."*
The central MEMORY line. A Rough Draft is the truth, hunted; it evolves by
being processed — each stage is the same document with less of what happened
in it. The Corrected Record is pristine, perforated where names used to be.
Signature: **Revision** (replaces the target's last move with nothing — the
move is "no longer on the record" for 3 turns).
Stats: 42/40/42/58 → 60/62/64/60 → 82/84/88/62. ◆ Final stage appears wild
only after the player has accepted ≥1 Corrected Version in the Ledger.
**Visual**: pencil-marked pages, marginalia alive and squirming → typed
memo, half the margins gone quiet → an immaculate white file that hums,
black redaction bars drifting across it like fish.

### 16 MEMORY HOLE — MEMORY (rare)
*"The archive is clean because someone got there first."*
Not an absence — a *designed* absence. Found where records should be.
Signature: **Clean Archive** (removes all buffs, debuffs, and hazards from
the field; deals damage equal to what was forgotten).
Stats: 70/66/70/44.
**Visual**: a filing-cabinet drawer open onto perfect starless black; the
label is blank in a way that looks expensive.

### 17 FAVOR → 18 DEBT → 19 DEPENDENCY — MONEY
*"Access first. Dependence second. Ownership after that."* /
*"Some men do not buy influence. They build dependence."*
Garnier's logic as a life cycle. A Favor is charming — warm handshake,
no visible strings. The strings are simply not visible yet.
Signature: **Make Departure Expensive** (target cannot SWAP while active).
Stats: 40/42/44/60 → 62/64/68/56 → 86/88/94/48. ◆ Debt→Dependency triggers
inside donor rooms only.
**Visual**: a gift box with a faint cursive tag ("no rush") → the same box
fused to a ribcage of ledger lines → a vast soft armchair whose shadow is a
server rack; sitting figures are part of the upholstery.

### 20 FINE PRINT — MONEY
*"A donation is not a coup... The file becomes legible only when the steps
are read together."*
Six-point type with appetite. Lives at the bottom of generous documents.
Signature: **Clause** (delayed damage, lands two turns later).
Stats: 50/62/46/54.
**Visual**: a centipede of microscopic text crawling off a contract's last
page, each segment a subclause, asterisks for eyes.

### 21 INVOICE — MONEY
*"People failing upward while the people below them inherit the invoice."*
The bill always arrives — just never upstairs. It knows your name.
Signature: **Pass The Cost** (transfers its own damage taken to the
opposing side's freshest combatant).
Stats: 56/70/40/62.
**Visual**: a windowed envelope hovering like a wasp; through the cellophane
window, your name in a font you never chose.

### 22 DRAFT NOTICE → 23 SELECTED → 24 SPENT — WAR
*"The machine does not ask who is guilty. It asks who can be processed."* /
*"Who gets protected? Who gets processed? Who gets spent?"* /
*"The country calls it service. The file calls it selection."*
The Draft Machine's product line. A Draft Notice slides out of an old printer
under fluorescent light. The red SELECTED stamp is its adult form. What
remains afterward is Spent: a folded flag's worth of paperwork that still
answers roll call.
Signature: **Selected Stamp** (WAR; marks target — marked targets take +50%
from OFFICE and WAR).
Stats: 44/52/40/58 → 66/78/56/64 → 80/98/64/56. ◆ Selected→Spent is
story-only (the game makes you watch the cost; it is never a power-up the
player celebrates).
**Visual**: a government form mid-extrusion from a printer, edges
fluorescent-lit → the same form upright, one wet red SELECTED stamp for a
face → a neat stack of forms bound in a folded triangle, name field worn
illegible by handling.

### 25 EMERGENCY SPEED — WAR
*"The language of emergency always wants to hurry the room. Speed is useful
because it makes moral questions look like delays."*
A klaxon with a clock for a heart. Wherever it appears, deliberation dies.
Signature: **Hurry The Room** (all combatants' Tempo doubled; accuracy of
careful (non-STAB) moves drops — scrutiny becomes bad manners).
Stats: 46/58/38/92.
**Visual**: a red rotary siren atop spindly scaffold legs, stenciled
TEMPORARY MEASURE, the paint long since permanent.

### 26 X9R — WAR/MONEY (rare)
*"Tecca x9R battery fire · 32 dead in Des Moines."* /
*"An electric fire occurred from a faulty battery, killing 32 workers."*
The casualty ledger with a product number. Found only at the Des Moines site.
It burns at exactly the temperature the press release said was impossible.
Signature: **Faulty By Design** (heavy damage; recoil — the cost is always
shared downward).
Stats: 72/92/58/60.
**Visual**: a sleek consumer battery cell, branding intact, venting thin
elegant smoke; the smoke forms 32 brief signatures before dispersing.

### 27 FORM 28 — OFFICE
*"Doctrine: 28th Amendment martial-law power."* /
*"The memo does not explain the plot. It authorizes it."*
An amendment with a body. It does not argue; it cites itself.
Signature: **Authorization** (its next move ignores immunities and Room
Pressure — it is, after all, legal now).
Stats: 64/60/74/40.
**Visual**: a marble-white legal document standing like a stele, seal
embossed too deep, articles numbered in a column that doesn't end at the
page's edge.

### 28 PROTECTED FAILURE — OFFICE
*"One reckless man is dangerous. A protected reckless man is government."* /
*"A coming of age story in a country that keeps mistaking protected failure
for leadership."*
The apex predator of the ineptocracy: failure that cannot fall. Six
bankruptcies orbit it like moons.
Signature: **Fail Upward** (when its Resolve drops below half, its Pressure
and Insulation *rise* — consequence slides off and becomes promotion).
Stats: 78/64/86/36.
**Visual**: an overstuffed executive chair ascending a staircase of broken
rungs; medals pinned to upholstery; no occupant visible, only the impression
of weight.

### 29 NEUROCLICK — MEMORY/MONEY (legendary-class)
*"NeuroClick brain chip · marketed as 'cures paralysis'."* /
*"It gives forgetting a product shape... erasure feel merciful."*
The business model of forgetting, with a clean interface. Boss of Act III;
filing it requires Sabrina's arc and Mark's signal-jam.
Signature: **Curated Relief** (offers the target's side a heal; if accepted,
overwrites one of that side's Ledger pages — mechanical mercy with a price).
Stats: 88/84/82/70.
**Visual**: a coin-sized implant of surgical white and warm UI-blue, haloed
by soft loading dots; its shadow on the wall is a filing cabinet.

### 30 THE DRAFT MACHINE — WAR/OFFICE (legendary-class)
*"The machine breaks people before anyone names the villain."*
The whole apparatus, encountered in D.C. It has no face — it has a queue.
Signature: **Processing** (each turn, adds a Draft Notice token to the field;
the player must argue the queue down before reaching the core).
Stats: 96/88/90/44.
**Visual**: a corridor-sized assemblage of printers, stamp arms, and
fluorescent tubes arranged like a ribcage; forms move through it with the
patience of digestion.

### 31 THE ROOM — MONEY/OFFICE (legendary-class)
*"The mansion is not a house. It is a position."* /
*"Sometimes they enter a room where silence has already been designed as the
easiest behavior."*
The final architecture. The mansion's interior as a single organism: it
ranks everyone who enters. Fought as the Act IV finale *around* Garnier —
because Garnier is never the monster; he owns the monster's lease.
Signature: **Own The Door** (WITHDRAW disabled; each turn the Room's
hospitality offers the player Convenience in exchange for Ledger pages).
Stats: 110/90/104/40.
**Visual**: a paneled study breathing slowly; windows glowing like server
racks; chairs arranged so that one of them is subtly a throne and none of
them are for you.

## Distribution notes

- WITNESS species are **never wild**. They are earned through people: gifts,
  bonds, quests. The machine spawns; the network is built.
- MEDIA/MONEY dominate early urban zones; WAR rises in Act II; MEMORY in
  Act III; OFFICE everywhere there is a corridor.
- Legendary-class Manifestations are story-gated, one each per act.
- Wild Manifestations appear in **pressure patches** (the canon line "The map
  is not geography. It is pressure" is the encounter system's design brief).
